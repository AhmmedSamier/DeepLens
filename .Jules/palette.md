## 2025-05-18 - Actionable Empty States
**Learning:** When a search yields no results, users often hit a dead end. Providing a contextual action (like "Rebuild Index") directly in the empty state transforms frustration into a recovery path.
**Action:** Always look for ways to make "empty" or "error" states actionable, especially when the recovery action is known and available.

## 2025-05-18 - Keyboard Discoverability in Tooltips
**Learning:** Power users rely on keyboard shortcuts but often forget them. Exposing shortcuts (e.g., "(/t)") in tooltips for clickable elements creates a natural learning path without cluttering the UI.
**Action:** Audit all icon-only buttons to ensuring their tooltips include associated keyboard shortcuts or slash commands.

## 2024-05-24 - [Empty State Actionability]
**Learning:** Highlighting "Rebuild Index" in empty states is tricky. Users might click it unnecessarily.
**Action:** Adding "(Fix missing files)" to the tooltip clarifies intent without cluttering the UI.
